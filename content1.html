Morality of Modern Game

There are many modern games let players make moral decision while game playing. However, most of the games have a shallow choice that cannot reflect the real world.

Moreover, the studies show that viedo game will impact on people's behaviors. Therefore, making a right content of video game is very important, and that is duty for game designers.

Indeed, a lot of games try to aviod some of immoral behaviors in another way. For example, in Grand Thft Auto, a violence related game, does not have any children characters.

On the other hand, some games are using immoral or even illeal actions as their selling point. Some of them have to be banned in many countries.
